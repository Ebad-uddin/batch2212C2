Blueberry Ginger Smoothie

Info:

Combine blueberries, ginger, yogurt, and honey for a soothing and anti-inflammatory blend.

Benefits:

Ginger aids digestion, while blueberries bring antioxidants and a touch of sweetness.

Duration:

About 5 minutes

Servings:

1 serving

Ingredients:

Blueberries, fresh ginger, plain yogurt, milk, honey (optional).

Recipe:

Blend 1 cup blueberries, a small slice of peeled ginger, 1/2 cup plain yogurt, 1/2 cup milk, and honey until well combined.
